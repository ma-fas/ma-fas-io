About

Personal portfolio || Magdalena Fas

Hi there. My name is Magdalena.

How it all came to be...

I used to be many things in my life: I mixed coctails in Spanish chiringuitos, analysed air route networks, dispatched refrigerated trucks all across Europe, turned planes around, sold high-end trips to Costa Rica... You get the idea. In late 2015, I decided front-end was the way to go and started self-studying. A couple of weeks down the line, I sought some structure to my learning, and in doing so I followed the typical newcomer's path that lead through what most likely amounted to half of this world's MOOCs. I soon found the overwhelming choice frustrating and reverted to smaller-scale private projects.

What can I do for you?

I feel pretty comfortable writing HTML, CSS and JQuery code. I continue to learn pure JS, throwing some libraries in here and there. I have made initial contact with Ajax calls, AngularJS, Meteor and React frameworks, as well as Ionic for hybrid app dev. I am nowhere near a pro in any of these, but ready to learn. On the CSS front, I work confidently with Sass/SCSS and Bootstrap. In my projects I use npm, bower and Gulp. I also have basic knowledge working with Git. I can cut PSD files and do intermediate graphics and photo manipulation using either Gimp or Adobe suite. I worked on a content-heavy Wordpress-based site and am fully aware of SEO issues. I have general knowledge of web analytics and UI/UX.

How about my soft skills?

I worked in data analysis, I coordinated teams, I am good dealing with people (and yes, this includes customers). I know how to look for and find information. I have spent nearly seven years studying and working in London, I am also a confident speaker of Spanish (three years of residence in Spain) and German; my mother tongue is Polish. I have significant experience working in an international environment as well as multinational teams. I like to believe I have a good eye for design.

Where do I want to take it next?

JS, JS, JS. Frameworks. Unit testing. But above all, I feel this is the right time to take the leap into professional environment. I am passionate, I enjoy putting those puzzles together by myself, but I would very much appreciate some guidance and the flow of real-life projects. Don't ask me where I want to be in five years' time, better tell me how far can you help me get by then.
